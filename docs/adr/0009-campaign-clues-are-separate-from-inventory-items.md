# Campaign clues are separate from inventory items

Campaign Clues form a campaign-owned model separate from generic Items, with exclusive, revocable grants to Campaign Characters and an immutable, GM-private custody history alongside the current grant. Character inventory read models compose granted clues as zero-load entries instead of persisting them as ordinary inventory rows. Existing campaign-scoped document items are migrated once and the legacy `document` item kind is then removed. Archived clues can be restored for 60 days; expiry permanently purges their content and image while preserving only a minimal GM-private tombstone and custody history. This boundary preserves clue secrecy, supports distinct text and image content rules and a future Clue Board, and avoids spreading clue-specific authorization and lifecycle branches throughout the generic item model.
